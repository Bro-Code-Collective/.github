### 🔥 What we're about

We're two undergrad devs who turn 3 AM chaos into shipped code. No fancy roadmap, no perfect process — just ideas we're curious about, bugs we refuse to lose to, and a habit of somehow pushing something real out the other end.

```
> status: awake at unreasonable hours
> current_mood: debugging
> projects_shipped: more than we expected
> projects_broken_first: all of them
```

### 🛠️ How we roll

- 💡 **Idea today, repo tomorrow** — we don't let good ideas sit
- 🐛 **Break it, then fix it** — that's basically our QA process
- 🌙 **3AM is a valid work hour** — some of our best commits prove it
- 🚀 **Ship > perfect** — working code beats a beautiful plan every time
